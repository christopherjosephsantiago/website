Christopher Joseph Santiago

Christopher, best known as Chris, is a polymath in many facets of creative outlets. From photography and music, to cooking, graphic design, and even coding, Chris' interests and endeavors are far more vast that can be simply explained on an about section. His academic achievements matches that of his creative outputs. Attaining a Masters Degree in Demographic and Social Analysis during his graduate studies and a double major in Psychology & Social Behavior and Criminology with minor in Film & Media Studies during his undergraduate studies, Christopher accels in education and research paths. Academically, Chris is most well known for his thesis and research regarding the social connection of music through social media. This research implements Social Network Analysis while using a large Twitter database, being able to capture and test target demographics based on a user's location and listening preferences (based on tweets). Creatively, Chris has a foot in every direction of which can be explored on this very page.
